# Proxies

## Supported formats

> [!NOTE]
> 
> Hostnames are supported, in addition to IPs by our proxy parser. However, some solvers may not support hostnames.
>
> `http://` proxies are also supported.

- `ip`
- `ip:port`
- `ip:port:user:pass`
- `ip:port@user:pass`
- `user:pass:ip:port`
- `user:pass@ip:port`
